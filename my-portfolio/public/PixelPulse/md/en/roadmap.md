# Pixel Pulse Roadmap {: data-toc-key="top" }

Welcome to the Pixel Pulse roadmap! Our mission is to build the most reliable, beautiful, and intuitive sound meter and hearing health coach for Android. This document outlines our recent launches and future directions.

We believe in continuous improvement. Your feedback is crucial to this process, so if you have ideas or suggestions, we'd love to hear them!

This is the powerful foundation of Pixel Pulse, available now.

### **The Core Experience**
- **Real-Time Meter:** A beautiful, expressive gauge with dynamic coloring and live Min/Avg/Max statistics.
- **Session History & Playback:** Robust local database for saving, searching, and interactively replaying recording sessions.
- **Advanced UI/UX:** A fully featured history screen with multi-select, a contextual action bar, and a detailed session view.
- **Microphone Calibration:** A simple tool for all users to apply a dB offset for higher accuracy.
- **Core Settings:** A complete settings suite including "Keep Screen On" and dynamic theming.

### **The Awareness Engine (Pixel Pulse+)**
- **Full Sound Exposure Analysis:** A dedicated screen with automatic background monitoring to generate daily, weekly, and monthly trend charts.
- **Weekly Noise Budget:** A proactive tool to help users manage their exposure to loud environments based on WHO guidelines.
- **Proactive Noise Alerts:** A customizable notification system for hearing safety.
- **Professional Tools:** Advanced features including A/C/Z Frequency Weighting, CSV data export, adjustable meter speed, and ambient haptic feedback.
- **Billing Integration:** A simple, one-time purchase to unlock all Plus features permanently.

Looking further ahead, these are some of the concepts we are exploring to make Pixel Pulse even better.

### **Home Screen Integration**
- **Pixel Pulse+ Widgets:** Design and build a suite of beautiful, configurable home screen widgets that display real-time or recent average dB levels.

### **Advanced Analysis & Usability**
- **Chart Interactions:** Add the ability to tap or long-press on chart bars to see the exact value.
- **Session Tagging & Notes:** Allow users to add custom tags (e.g., #concert, #work) or notes to saved sessions for better organization and search.

### **Wear OS Companion**
- **Standalone Watch App:** Create a fully independent Wear OS app that uses the watch's microphone for on-the-go measurements.
- **Complications & Tiles:** Develop a Complication for at-a-glance readings on the watch face and a Tile for quick access to the meter.
